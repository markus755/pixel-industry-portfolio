Shopping concept

Client: vertical GmbH

Challenge:

Show a selection of services for example device as a service, software as a service etc. that an Admin can easily see the details, assign, and manage the service afterwards.

Solution:

Create a kind of shop system to show all the services. Every service has also a detail page where the user often can choose between different option and the possibility to rent or buy the service. To get a good experience is the whole system responsive as well.
